# Aerobic exercise and depression risk

Produced by Gemini Deep Research. Exercise is widely recommended for mental health. Many studies
show that people who exercise regularly tend to be less depressed, and most experts agree it
helps. Aerobic exercise like running or cycling is especially good. While we can't be 100%
certain it prevents depression, the overall evidence is positive and exercise is a safe,
low-cost option that almost everyone can benefit from.
